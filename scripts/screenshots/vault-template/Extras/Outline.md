Coastal Light — working outline

**Act One — Departure (Ch. 1-3)**
Mara returns to Kestrel Point after her father goes silent. Reconnects with the town, finds the keeper's log, begins to see her father differently than the version of him she's carried for twelve years.

**Act Two — Storm Warning (Ch. 4-7)**
A major storm strands them on the island together. The Marguerite runs into trouble on the shoal; Mara sounds the fog signal by hand and helps guide the boat to safety. Aftermath forces the automation conflict into the open.

**Act Three — The Last Light (Ch. 8-?)**
Mara chooses to stay and train as assistant keeper, confronting the trust board directly. Book ends on the light continuing its rotation — open enough for a sequel if the automation fight escalates.
