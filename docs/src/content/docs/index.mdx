## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Multiple Projects" icon="puzzle">
    Create and manage independent writing projects with their own configurations. Switch between projects seamlessly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Word Counting" icon="list-format">
    Real-time hierarchical word counts at file, folder, and project levels. Track your progress as you write.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content vs Source" icon="open-book">
    Designate folders as "content" (counts toward goals) or "source" (research/reference). Focus on what matters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Writing Stats Panel" icon="bars">
    Persistent sidebar showing current file, folder, and project word counts with session and daily tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Project Dashboard" icon="chart">
    View comprehensive project statistics, manage projects, and track progress toward your writing goals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible Organization" icon="setting">
    No rigid folder structures required. Organize your writing however works best for you.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Who is Lighthouse for?

- **Novelists** working on long-form fiction with multiple drafts and research materials
- **Academic writers** managing theses, dissertations, or research papers
- **Technical writers** coordinating documentation projects
- **Content creators** managing multiple writing projects simultaneously
- **Anyone** who needs professional writing project management in Obsidian

## Why Lighthouse?

> "It was done; it was finished. Yes, she thought, laying down her brush in extreme fatigue, I have had my vision."
> — Virginia Woolf, *To the Lighthouse*

Lighthouse brings the clarity and focus of dedicated writing apps like Ulysses to Obsidian, without sacrificing any of Obsidian's power and flexibility. Your writing stays in standard markdown files in your vault—no vendor lock-in, no proprietary formats.
