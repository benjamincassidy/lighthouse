## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="The Writing Workspace" icon="rocket">
    One command opens a dedicated Library and Inspector layout — Obsidian's native file explorer steps aside for the duration, so it feels like a purpose-built writing app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Groups & Extras" icon="open-book">
    Organize each project into nestable Groups with custom icons, plus a built-in Extras group for research and notes that's automatically excluded from word counts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Word Counting" icon="list-format">
    Real-time hierarchical counts at file, group, and project levels. Per-file and per-group goals with inline progress rings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deadline Pacing" icon="approve-check">
    Set a target finish date and see your required daily word count recalculate as you write. Rolling 7-day average shows whether you're on pace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Writing Activity Heatmap" icon="bars">
    GitHub-style 13-week calendar showing daily output intensity. Writing streaks, personal best, and rest day tracking built in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Export to PDF, DOCX & EPUB" icon="document">
    Compile a project into a single manuscript with built-in style presets, citation formatting, and table of contents generation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Split & Merge Notes" icon="pencil">
    Split a note at the cursor into a new sibling file, or merge notes back together — reshape a project's structure without leaving the editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flow Mode" icon="setting">
    Distraction-free writing with one command. Hides sidebars, tabs, ribbon, and status bar. Optional typewriter scroll and custom font settings.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Who is Lighthouse for?

- **Novelists** working on long-form fiction with multiple drafts and research materials
- **Academic writers** managing theses, dissertations, or research papers
- **Technical writers** coordinating documentation projects
- **Content creators** managing multiple writing projects simultaneously
- **Anyone** who needs professional writing project management in Obsidian

## Why Lighthouse?

> "It was done; it was finished. Yes, she thought, laying down her brush in extreme fatigue, I have had my vision."
> — Virginia Woolf, *To the Lighthouse*

Lighthouse brings the clarity and focus of dedicated writing apps like Ulysses to Obsidian, without sacrificing any of Obsidian's power and flexibility. Your writing stays in standard markdown files in your vault—no vendor lock-in, no proprietary formats.
